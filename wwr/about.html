Splash White Water Rafting

The mission of Splash White Water Rafting is to connect people with the untamed beauty and thrilling power of the river, fostering not just memorable adventures, but also deep respect for nature and a commitment to safety. From providing top-tier, certified guides to maintaining the highest quality equipment, we ensure every trip whether a calm family float or a challenging whitewater expeditionis executed with professionalism and passion.

Founded in 1998 by veteran river guide Maya Chen, Splash White Water Rafting started with one raft and a passion for sharing the exhilarating rapids of the Kolob River Gorge.

History

Over two decades, we've grown into the region's premier rafting company, expanding our expeditions across three major river systems while always maintaining our commitment to local, highly-trained guides and delivering unforgettable wilderness experiences.
